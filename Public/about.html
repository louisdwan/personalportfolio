About Me

I'm a Business Systems Analyst and Project Manager with a passion for leveraging technology to drive efficiency and innovation within organizations. My background is rooted in finance and information systems, and I've had the opportunity to work on diverse projects across several industries.

My career has been focused on optimizing business processes and implementing enterprise solutions. I have experience working with platforms like NetSuite, Coupa, and Oracle Financials, and I've led projects that have streamlined operations across Supply Chain, Procure-to-Pay, and other core financial functions. Whether it's managing a large-scale NetSuite implementation or developing automation solutions, I'm driven by the challenge of improving efficiency and creating impactful solutions.

Outside of my professional endeavors, I enjoy exploring Ireland's scenic hiking trails and experimenting with new recipes in the kitchen. Traveling fuels my curiosity, and I’m always eager to learn new languages. When I’m not on the move, you’ll find me playing golf or working on exciting new projects.

I am always eager to connect and discuss new opportunities. Please don't hesitate to reach out!
